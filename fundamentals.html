FUNDAMENTALS

Focus: Building Core Technical Skills

From the first shot to the highest levels, the Whole Player Development Pathway (WPDP) provides recommendations for various development stages and pathways. The Fundamentals stage focuses on developing core technical skills, establishing proper contact points, and building consistent rally patterns. Players learn to execute basic strokes with improved control and understanding.

How to Spot This Level

  • Players can rally 3–6 shots consistently with modified equipment
  • Learning proper contact point and early racquet preparation
  • Developing basic forehand and backhand swing patterns
  • Beginning to understand court positioning and movement
  • Players typically ages 7–10 years old
